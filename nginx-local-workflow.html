Local Micro-Frontend Proxy

Engineered a local reverse proxy using Nginx to unify an ecosystem of 7+ disparate micro-frontends during development. Configured port mapping and route-based proxying via nginx.conf, enabling developers to traverse the entire site (Home, Catalogue, Cart, Checkout, etc.) on a single domain and eliminating the complex overhead of manually managing multiple local ports.
